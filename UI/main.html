Our AI Technology

EcoTrack AI uses cutting-edge machine learning techniques to analyze energy consumption patterns across different building types and usage scenarios. Our system goes beyond simple regression models to understand complex relationships between usage factors.

The prediction engine combines multiple advanced approaches:

  • Time-series forecasting with LSTM neural networks
  • Feature importance analysis for key consumption drivers
  • Anomaly detection to identify inefficiencies
  • Hybrid models combining statistical and machine learning approaches

This comprehensive approach ensures you get the most accurate predictions and actionable recommendations.
